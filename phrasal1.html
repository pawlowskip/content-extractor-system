Phrasal Verbs - obowiązkowe

ask out zaprosić kogoś (do kina, restauracji) You behave like a moron! Ask her out and then you will know if she likes you or not!
break down załamywać się, kończyć się niepowodzeniem The discussion broke down because nobody wanted to accept any compromise.
break down zepsuć się They got caught quite easily as their runaway car broke down after few miles.
bring up wychować She was brought up to respect other people.
calm down uspokoić się They had a hard time in their relationship but now everything seems to have calmed down.
care for opiekować się, troszczyć She cares for her younger sister.
check in zgłosić się do odprawy All the passengers should check in one hour before the departure.
check out sprawdzać Can somebody check out who is at the door?
check out zdać pokój (w hotelu), wymeldować się She checked out early in the morning and continued her journey.
clean up posprzątać Clean up your room before you leave.
clear up przejaśniać się (o pogodzie), przechodzić (o chorobie) The weather's clearing up.
come across sth/sb natknąć się (na coś/kogoś) I came across this precious painting while I was tidying my basement.
come back wracać Don't wait for me, I don't know when I'll be coming back.
count on liczyć na kogoś You can always count on Jim in situation like this.
cross out skreślić, wykreślić Your essay is too long - cross some sentences out.
decide on zdecydować się na coś We have decided on holiday in Spain.
depend on polegać na You can always depend on me. The tram is late again! You really can't depend on public transport.
depend on sth zależeć (od czegoś) Our decision depends on the results of the test.
do without radzić sobie bez czegoś I'm afraid she'll have to do without my help.
drop off podrzucić kogoś gdzieś po drodze I'm going to the city centre now, so if you want I can drop you off to the library.
fall behind zostawać z tyłu The horse started the race quite promising but then fell behind on the last bend. Tom has fallen behind in his English recently.
fill (sth) up wypełnić, zapełnić The room was filled up with people.
fill in sth wypełniać Please fill in the application form.
find out dowiedzieć się Did you find out how much he had paid?
focus on/upon koncentrować się His research focuses on developing new information technologies.
get back wracać When did you get back?
get in (into) wsiadać, wchodzić How did all of you manage to get into such a small car? The doors were closed and I couldn't get in.
get off (sth) wysiadać, wychodzić To get to the museum you have to get off at fifth stop. What time do you usually get off work?
get on wsiadać do/na The bus was full so we couldn't get on.
get out wynosić się Get out of my house!
get out wysiadać I got out of the car and went home.
get over dochodzić do siebie, pogodzić się z czymś It took him over two years to get over his wife's death.
get over znaleźć rozwiązanie problemu There were many technical problems but they got over it.
get sth back dostać coś z powrotem Can I borrow this book? You'll get it back next week.
get through dodzwonić się I tried to contact her but I couldn't get through.
get together (with sb) spotykać się Let's get together and talk about it.
get up wstawać (z łóżka) Mike always gets up at 7 o'clock.
give back oddawać Please give me back my CDs.
give up sth przestać coś robić I gave up smoking two months ago.
go away wyjeżdżać, iść (sobie) Leave me alone! Go away!
go back wracać When are you going back to Madrit?
go on kontynuować, trwać Sorry for the interruption, please go on.
go out gasnąć The light went out as we sat for dinner.
go out wychodzić, spotykać się z ludźmi Mark doesn't go out much.
go out wychodzić, wyjeżdżać Let's go out for a walk.
go with sth towarzyszyć, dziać się jako skutek czegoś Riots usually go with unnecessary bloodshed.
grow up dorastać Don't behave like a child! Grow up!
hang on czekać przez chwilę Hang on a second, I'll be right there.
head for iść/jechać w określonym kierunku, zmierzać do The troops were heading for the capital city.
hear from otrzymywać od kogoś wiadomość I haven't heard from him for ages.
hold on czekać chwilę Hold on a second, I'm almost ready.
hurry up pośpieszyć się Hurry up! We have to be there at 5.
join in przyłączać się We're going for a picnic tomorrow. Why don't you join in?
keep away trzymać się z daleka od czegoś Keep away from him or you'll get into trouble.
keep on kontynuować, robić coś dalej, bez przerwy Why you always keep on asking me about Mark? I don't want to talk about him!
know of wiedzieć o czymś, znać Do you know of any Italian restaurants in Paris?
laugh at śmiać się z czegoś What are you laughing at?
lay off zwalniać Many people working in this factory were laid off without previous notice.
leave out opuszczać, pomijać Prepare the list carefully, I don't want anyone to be left out.
let down zawodzić You let me down again, I can't trust you any more.
log in/on zalogować się, włączyć do sieci I can't log in, I forgot the password.
log out/off wylogować się, wyłączyć się z sieci Please log out and turn the computer off.
look after opiekować się, pilnować We need to find someone to look after the kids when we're out tomorrow evening.
look around rozglądać się Look around and check if you find something interesting for you.
look at przyglądać się, patrzeć What are you looking at?
look for szukać I'm looking for a room to rent. Do you know of any in this area?
look forward to cieszyć sie na coś, bardzo czekać na I'm looking forward to hearing from you soon.
look out uważać Look out!
look up sprawdzać Could someone look up this word in a dictionary?
make up robić makijaż I always make up before I leave house.
match up pasować, zgadzać się They will have to be interrogated once again as their stories proved not to match up.
move in wprowadzać się I didn't like this flat when I moved in but now I think it's quite cosy.
move out wyprowadzać się We have to move out till the end of the month.
name after nazwać po kimś Jacky was named after her grandmother.
note down notować/zapisywać Please note down the following emergency numbers.
pick up podnosić Sue picked up the phone and dialled her boyfriend's number.
put off odkładać, przełożyć na inny termin Can you put off the meeting? I need to talk to you.
put on zakładać (na siebie) It's quite cold so you'd better put on your coat.
rely on/upon polegać na He relies on her common sense in situations like this one.
result in skutkować His negligence resulted in severe health problems.
ring up dzwonić, telefonować Ring me up in the evening.
run away uciekać Run away or they'll beat you up.
run out kończyć się, brakować We've run out of the paper for printing. Time's running out, we've got only 2 minutes left. I ran out of butter in the morning so I had to go shopping.
save up zaoszczędzić I want to save up for a new computer.
set off wyruszyć, rozpocząć podróż We set off early in the morning.
slow down zwalniać You're driving too fast, please slow down.
switch off wyłączyć Switch off the radio before you leave.
switch on włączać Switch on the TV, there's weather forecast in few minutes.
take away zabierać, odbierać Mary took away a knife from her child.
take back oddawać, zwracać Adam took his new trousers back as he discovered a flaw on them.
take off wystartować When I was young I loved watching planes take off.
take off zdejmować, ściągać He was so tired that he didin't even take his clothes off.
take to polubić I took to her the moment I saw her smile.
take up brać/zabierać się za coś Sue's taken up basketball recently.
throw away wyrzucać When will you finally throw away this old coat?
touch down lądować The plane touched down safely and on time.
try on przymierzać Try on the red blouse, I think it suits better.
turn back zawracać After two days we had no money and had to turn back.
turn off wyłączać Turn off the radio please, I'd like to read.
turn on włączać Turn on the TV, the game starts in few minutes.
wake up obudzić się He woke up too late and had to leave without breakfast.
wash up zmywać I hate washing up.
watch out uważać Watch out! You are not allowed to walk here without proper equipment!
write down zapisywać Would you like to write down my address?
